About

Hi! I'm Matthew Ragland, a computer science major at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. This site is a showcase of the projects I've worked on, and academic achievements I have made.

Computer programming has always fascinated me. My first experience with coding was in 7th grade. As a part of a new program we were all allowed to choose a topic to study, and I chose programming. Although I started learning basic HTML and CSS, by the end of the year I had created a game similar to Pong from scratch. I loved the problem solving and creativity involved in the task, and I knew I should persue the interest further.

When I got the opportunity to take a computer science course in high school I jumped at the opportunity. I quickly realized this is what I wanted to have a career in, which led me to Rose.

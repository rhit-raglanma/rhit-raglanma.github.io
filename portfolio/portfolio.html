Portfolio

  • Cypher

    My largest high school project was a cypher app for Apple devices. I created an app that would take in text, and both encode or decode it. At first I started with a basic Caesar Cypher, but I wanted a larger challenge. After looking at different encoding methods online, I decided to create a Nihilst Cypher. This uses a string of 5 characters along with a message. Based on these characters, the message is encoded differently.

  • CSSE 220 final project

In addition to coding the game from scratch, we also created most of the visual art that was used in the game.
